About me:

  • Name: Martina
  • Occupation: BT furtHER trainee
  • Education: Cambridge University, Linguistics BA
  • Coding interests: Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning

Prior to commencing the BT furtHER course, I was completing my BA degree in Linguistics at Cambridge. My very first experience with coding was in my first year with the CF: G Introduction to Codin and thereafter, I self studied Python using online courses. In my second year I undertook an internship using Machine Learning in Python to research Native Language Identification. This internship was taken in the Cambridge University Computer Laboratory and sponsored by Cambridge Assessment English and this was the first time I put my skills into practice and worked alongside other people. In my final year, I then completed my dissertation titled 'A Diachronic Study of Multicultural London English using Word Embeddings.'Between graduating and this course, I completed courses on data analysis and deep learning which I am continuing to study.
I am currently halfway through the course and it has been so much fun so far!
